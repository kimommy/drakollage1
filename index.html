Drakollage

Drakollage is an independent handmade endeavor by Eryfili. Drakollage is about slow processes, attention to detail, welcomed mistakes and surprises, personalized wishes, and experiments. Each project is handled with care and consideration for the receiver and requires the same care. Every artwork used in the collages is carefully credited. Each project is created for the special order and therefore each one is unique. Currently, the collection includes notebooks, diaries, ashtrays, coasters, stickers, boxes, and bookmarks.

The maker

Behind drakollage is Eryfili. Eryfili is a Greek artist based in Athens who has studied dance and performance practices. In her artistic practice she works with photos, archives and found materials. Soon after her MA, she realized that she could extend her practice to other modes of production. Although as a hobby, she has been making collages on canvases since she was 18, she set up Drakollage in 2021. The first products, which continue to be part of the collection, included mostly notebooks, ashtrays and boxes that incorporated works of international artists. Today, as she develops her vision, she continues this work, but is mostly interested in sustainable and ethical handmade creations, and therefore, focuses mostly on binding notebooks herself, and using secondhand fabrics and other materials.
